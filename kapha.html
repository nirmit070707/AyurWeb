Kapha Dosha

The Energy of Structure and Stability

Elemental Constitution

Kapha is composed of the fundamental elements of Water and Earth, governing all structure, stability, and lubrication in the body and mind.

💧 Water
🌍 Earth

Physical Characteristics

Body Structure
Solid, heavy build with strong bones and well-developed muscles. Tendency toward larger frame and slower metabolism.
Skin & Hair
Smooth, moist, and thick skin that is cool to touch. Hair is typically thick, wavy, lustrous, and oily.
Energy Patterns
Steady, consistent energy with great endurance. Slow to start but maintains stamina for long periods.

Mental & Emotional Traits

Mental Qualities
Calm, steady thinking with excellent long-term memory. Methodical approach to learning and problem-solving.
Emotional Nature
Loving, compassionate, and patient by nature. Can experience attachment, possessiveness, and depression when imbalanced.
Communication
Slow, thoughtful speech with a melodious voice. Prefers to listen and speaks with consideration and depth.

Functions in the Body

Structure & Support
Provides structural integrity to bones, muscles, and tissues. Maintains body mass and physical strength.
Lubrication & Moisture
Governs all body fluids including synovial fluid in joints, mucus membranes, and cellular hydration.
Immunity & Protection
Regulates immune system function, wound healing, and provides natural resistance to disease and infection.
