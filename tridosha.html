Tridoshic Lifestyle Tips

🧘‍♀️ Harmony in Routine

Wake early, eat on time, and sleep by 10 PM. Routine is essential for preserving natural balance.

🍲 Balanced Diet

Favor freshly cooked meals with all six Ayurvedic tastes in moderation. Keep meals warm and easy to digest.

🌿 Seasonal Adaptation

Modify your routine and food according to seasonal changes. Stay warm in winter, cool in summer, and grounded in dry seasons.

🕉️ Mindful Practices

Engage in daily meditation, yoga, and pranayama to maintain emotional and mental clarity.

Tridoshic-Friendly Foods

Favor These

🍚
Grains: Basmati rice, oats, barley, quinoa
🥦
Vegetables: Cooked carrots, greens, zucchini
🍒
Fruits: Ripe apples, berries, pears
🌰
Fats: Ghee, olive oil (moderate use)
🧂
Spices: Cumin, coriander, fennel
🍵
Drinks: Warm teas (ginger, tulsi), lemon water

Avoid/Minimize

❄️
Extreme Foods: Cold, raw, excessively spicy or oily items
🥓
Heavy Items: Fried or heavily processed food
🧊
Cold Drinks: Ice water or sodas
⏰
Irregular Eating: Skipping meals or eating too late

Common Imbalances & Remedies

Overstimulation & Burnout

Symptoms: Fatigue, anxiety, irritability

  • Prioritize rest and deep sleep
  • Warm herbal teas (ashwagandha, chamomile)
  • Abhyanga (oil massage before bed)

Digestive Upset

Symptoms: Gas, bloating, indigestion

  • Warm, simple meals with digestive spices
  • Regular mealtimes
  • Fennel or ginger tea after meals

Seasonal Mood Swings

Symptoms: Moodiness, fatigue, dry skin

  • Adjust lifestyle with seasons
  • Stay hydrated and moisturized
  • Gentle morning sun exposure
