Common Imbalances & Remedies

Overstimulation & Burnout

Symptoms: Fatigue, anxiety, irritability

  • Prioritize rest and deep sleep
  • Warm herbal teas (ashwagandha, chamomile)
  • Abhyanga (oil massage before bed)

Digestive Upset

Symptoms: Gas, bloating, indigestion

  • Warm, simple meals with digestive spices
  • Regular mealtimes
  • Fennel or ginger tea after meals

Seasonal Mood Swings

Symptoms: Moodiness, fatigue, dry skin

  • Adjust lifestyle with seasons
  • Stay hydrated and moisturized
  • Gentle morning sun exposure
