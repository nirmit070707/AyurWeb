Morning Routine (5:00-7:00 AM)

Wake up early without snoozing. Immediately get moving with vigorous exercise like running or yoga. Take a warm shower followed by cool rinse. Practice energizing pranayama like Bhastrika. Avoid staying in bed too long.

Breakfast (7:00-8:00 AM)

Eat warm, spicy, and light foods. Include ginger tea, honey, and stimulating spices. Avoid cold, heavy, or oily foods. Eat smaller portions but ensure they are nutritious and warming to boost metabolism.

Work Hours (9:00 AM-6:00 PM)

Stay active and avoid sedentary periods. Take energizing breaks with movement. Challenge yourself mentally and physically. Maintain good posture and avoid excessive sitting. Keep the environment warm and well-lit.

Evening Routine (7:00-11:00 PM)

Have a light, early dinner with warming spices. Engage in stimulating conversations or activities. Practice dynamic meditation or dancing. Avoid heavy foods and excessive sleep. Stay active until bedtime.

⚠️ Common Kapha Imbalances & Remedies

  • Imbalance: Weight gain
    Remedy: Increase physical activity, favor light and dry foods like leafy greens, legumes, and spicy teas.
  • Imbalance: Congestion or sinus issues
    Remedy: Use warming spices like ginger, black pepper, and turmeric. Avoid dairy and cold drinks.
  • Imbalance: Lethargy & sluggish digestion
    Remedy: Start the day with hot lemon water, eat smaller meals, and avoid naps during the day.
  • Imbalance: Depression or emotional heaviness
    Remedy: Engage in stimulating activities, wake up before sunrise, and avoid overeating or emotional eating.
  • Imbalance: Water retention or swelling
    Remedy: Reduce salt intake, drink herbal teas (like dandelion root), and practice regular dry brushing.
