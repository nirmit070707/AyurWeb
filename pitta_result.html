Daily Routine for Pitta Balance

🌅

Early Morning

5:30 AM is the perfect time to rise. Start with lukewarm water on face and gentle stretches.

🌅

Body Movements

Practice cooling breath Yoga, quiet exercises like walking and swimming are beneficial.

🧘

Exercises

Focus on solar plexus, Moon Salutation help pacify Pitta. Avoid inverted poses.

🥗

Food

Some brekfast is recommended. Heavy lunch can be done as pitta being active in the afternoon.

💼

Work Balance

Take regular breaks. Stay hydrated. Avoid peak sun hours. Work in cool spaces.

😴

Early Sleep

Sleep by 10:00 PM. Use cooling oils for massage. Keep bedroom cool and dark.

Common Pitta Disorders & Home Remedies

Acidity & Heartburn

Symptoms: Burning sensation, sour taste, stomach discomfort

  • Drink cool coconut water daily
  • Chew fennel seeds after meals
  • Take 1 tsp aloe vera juice before meals
  • Avoid spicy and acidic foods
  • Eat smaller, frequent meals

Skin Irritations & Rashes

Symptoms: Redness, burning, inflammation, rashes

  • Apply cool aloe vera gel topically
  • Use coconut oil for massage
  • Take neem tablets or drink neem tea
  • Apply sandalwood paste for cooling
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure

Anger & Irritability

Symptoms: Short temper, frustration, aggressive behavior

  • Practice cooling pranayama (Sheetali)
  • Meditate daily for mental calm
  • Take brahmi or shankhpushpi herbs
  • Avoid conflicts during hot weather
  • Practice forgiveness and patience

Excessive Heat & Sweating

Symptoms: Profuse sweating, feeling overheated, hot flashes

  • Drink plenty of cool water
  • Eat cooling foods like cucumber
  • Apply cooling oils like coconut
  • Wear light-colored, loose clothing
  • Stay in cool, shaded environments

Liver & Digestive Issues

Symptoms: Poor digestion, liver heat, yellow discoloration

  • Drink fresh amla juice daily
  • Take turmeric with warm milk
  • Eat bitter gourd regularly
  • Drink dandelion tea
  • Follow a detox diet periodically

Migraines & Headaches

Symptoms: Severe headaches, sensitivity to light, nausea

  • Apply cool compress to forehead
  • Massage temples with peppermint oil
  • Practice gentle neck stretches
  • Stay hydrated with cool water
  • Rest in a dark, cool room
