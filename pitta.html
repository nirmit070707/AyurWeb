Pitta Dosha

The Energy of Transformation and Metabolism

Elemental Constitution

Pitta is composed of the fundamental elements of Fire and Water, governing all transformation, metabolism, and digestion in the body and mind.

Physical Characteristics

Body Structure
Medium build with good muscle definition. Well-proportioned frame with moderate weight and height.
Skin & Hair
Warm, soft skin that may be sensitive to sun. Hair is often fine, straight, and may gray or thin early.
Energy Patterns
Consistent, moderate energy levels with strong digestive fire. Regular appetite and good stamina.

Mental & Emotional Traits

Mental Qualities
Sharp intellect, focused thinking, and excellent analytical abilities. Natural leaders with strong decision-making skills.
Emotional Nature
Passionate, determined, and goal-oriented. Can experience anger, irritability, and impatience when imbalanced.
Communication
Clear, direct, and articulate speakers. Confident in expression with persuasive abilities.

Functions in the Body

Digestion & Metabolism
Controls digestive fire, enzyme production, and metabolic processes. Regulates body temperature and energy conversion.
Transformation
Governs all transformative processes including cellular metabolism, hormone production, and biochemical reactions.
Vision & Perception
Regulates eyesight, visual perception, and the ability to discriminate and judge situations clearly.
